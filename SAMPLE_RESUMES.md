## How to Use These Samples

1. **Copy the content** above for the desired role
2. **Create a Word document** or Google Doc
3. **Paste the content** and format it nicely
4. **Export/Save as PDF**
5. **Use in the application** for testing

- **Sample 1 (MERN)**: Should get 85-95% match for MERN Developer role
